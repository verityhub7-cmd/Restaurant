Since day one

Made fresh, served with a smile.

Wanna Eat started with one simple idea: comfort food should feel homemade, even when it's made fast. Every burger is pressed to order, every biryani simmered the slow way, and every shake blended thick — because flavor shouldn't be rushed.

What began as a handwritten menu on a notepad has grown into a full spread of burgers, wraps, biryani, chaat, and shakes — but the philosophy hasn't changed: fresh ingredients, honest portions, and a kitchen that cares.
